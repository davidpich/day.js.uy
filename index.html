JS Day is not just conference but a gathering, a celebration party where all the JS community meets and commemorates another great year.

An easy going event built for and by developers starting with a day of workshops and integration activities, followed by a conference day with plenty of time to chat, share experiences and make friends from the community between talks.

A unique experience for all 200 attendees with workshops and social events the first day and a single track conference the second day.
